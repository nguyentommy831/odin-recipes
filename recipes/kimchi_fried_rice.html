Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

This Simple, simple dish is super,super tasty. It's just a few ingredients, but it's a dish much loved by Koreans and a staple of our everyday lives

Ingredients:
  • Day Old Rice
  • Spam
  • Kimchi
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Egg
  • Cooking Oil
Steps:
  1. Heat up your oil of choice
  2. Add in spam and stir around until golden brown
  3. Then add in kimchi and rice
  4. Pour oyster sauce and a little bit of kimchi sauce and a little bit of soy sauce
  5. Mix around until rice is soft and everything is incorporated together
  6. Finally cook egg on separate pan and you have kimchi fried rice :)
